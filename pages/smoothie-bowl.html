Berry Smoothie Bowl

Refreshing, fruity, and packed with nutrients under $2!

This beautiful berry smoothie bowl is as delicious as it is Instagram-worthy. Quick to make and perfect for summer mornings!

Prep Time: 7 min   |   Servings: 1   |   Cost per Serving: ~$1.90

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup frozen mixed berries – $0.80
  • 1/2 banana – $0.20
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt or plant-based – $0.50
  • 1/4 cup oats – $0.10
  • 2 tbsp milk (or alt milk) – $0.10
  • Toppings: sliced fruit, granola, seeds – optional

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Blend berries, banana, yogurt, oats, and milk until smooth and thick.
  2. Pour into a bowl and smooth the surface.
  3. Top with your favorite fruits, granola, or seeds.
  4. Serve chilled and enjoy with a spoon!

💡 Tips & Tricks

Too thick? Add a splash of milk to loosen.
Meal prep: Make smoothie packs with pre-measured frozen ingredients.
